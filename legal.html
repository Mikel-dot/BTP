LEGAL NOTICE & POLICIES

Total transparency regarding your data, cookies, and our relationship with Riot Games.

1. General Information

BTP (Best Trick Pony) is an independent software project developed for analytical purposes and personal improvement within the League of Legends video game.

Contact for support or legal inquiries: contact@your-email.com

2. Privacy Policy (Software & Web)

On the website:

This website is strictly informative. It does not feature registration forms, nor does it collect personal data, IP addresses, or identifiable browsing information from users.

On the desktop application (BTP):

All data processing is performed locally on the user's computer. Credentials (API Keys) and match history are stored on the local hard drive and encrypted. BTP does not transmit any personal or account information to third-party servers, except for official requests made directly to Riot Games servers via their public API.

3. Cookie Policy

Why don't you see a cookie banner? Because we fully respect your privacy.

This website does not use tracking, analytical (such as Google Analytics), or advertising cookies. By not installing non-essential cookies on your browser, European regulations (GDPR) exempt us from the obligation to display a cookie acceptance banner. Your navigation here is completely anonymous.
