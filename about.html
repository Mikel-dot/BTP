TRANSPARENCY & SECURITY

Your PC, your data, your rules. Developed with absolute respect for the player and League of Legends' integrity.

🗄️ 100% Local Storage

Everything you do in BTP (champion pools, match notes, settings) is saved in local files on your own hard drive.

We do not have cloud servers storing your history. You have total control over your information.

🛡️ Game Integrity (TOS)

BTP is an analytical assistant, not a "cheat". We do not inject code (.dll), we do not read RAM, and we do not modify the client.

We exclusively use the official API and the LCU protocol legally provided by Riot Games.

🔐 Security Encryption

Any credentials needed to run the software are safely encrypted on your system.

BTP only uses this data to communicate directly and exclusively with official Riot Games servers.

🚫 Zero Hidden Telemetry

Your data is not our business model. BTP does not track your background behavior or sell player profiles.

The tool does exactly what it says, with no ghost processes consuming your resources while you play.

About the API Key usage

Currently, for the game-tracker to download your post-game stats, BTP requires the user to input a Development API Key from their Riot Developer account.

We are aware this adds an extra installation step, but it is a temporary measure exclusive to our Beta phase.

"Once BTP proves its value and Riot Games approves our Production Key, this requirement will completely disappear. The tool will become plug-and-play, operating silently without the player having to manage any keys."
