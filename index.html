Draft Assistant: Science, not Magic

BTP displays pure mathematical statistics extracted from High-ELO matches. We do not use "Black Box" AI algorithms.

We provide the empirical data so YOU, as an expert of your Champion Pool, can interpret the best course of action.

Train your Game Awareness

BTP's game-tracker is designed to improve your Macro-awareness. Grading your own objectives (Vision, CS, Map) seconds after the Nexus explodes is key to improvement.

Our core philosophy is Silent Self-Coaching.

The Player is the Pilot

We believe in user empowerment. BTP's goal is not to give you a strict set of rules to follow blindly.

BTP is a dynamic compass that helps you choose a logical option among all available possibilities based on empirical data.

⚠️ Important Development Note

We are currently in a Closed Beta phase. BTP has processed ONLY 10,000 matches for this prototype.

The statistical information shown is experimental. Please do not follow these numbers blindly in competitive games while we validate the model.

Build your Champion Pool

We firmly believe the best recommendation is based on what you actually know how to play.

Build your comfort list for your roles, and BTP will cross-reference the math with your real skill set.
