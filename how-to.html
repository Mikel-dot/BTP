HOW TO USE BTP

From zero to expert in three steps. Configure it once and let it work in the background while you focus on winning.

Step 1: Configure your API Key

Go to Settings or enter the Self-Coaching tab and click on "Riot API".

Input your Riot ID, Region, and the Development API Key generated in the Riot Developer portal.

You only have to do this once. Your key will be locally encrypted for future sessions.

Step 2: Define your Arsenal

Click on Pool Builder from the main menu.

Search for your comfort champions and add them to your list. BTP will use this information to prioritize picks during the draft phase.

Step 3: Play and Evaluate

Minimize BTP and enter a game. The draft assistant will open automatically upon finding a match.

When the Nexus explodes, BTP will launch a Mini-Popup. Take 10 seconds to grade your performance, and BTP will sync it with official stats.
