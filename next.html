WHAT'S NEXT?

Game-Tracking and Draft assistance are just the foundation. Our roadmap for upcoming updates aims to deepen the mathematical analysis of your matches.

Rune & Item Recommender

We will expand the Draft Assistant so it not only evaluates champions but also suggests optimal itemization in real-time.

Strictly based on the math of the current matchup and your team composition's scaling against the enemy. Goodbye generic builds; hello tactical adaptation.

Item Delta Analyzer

Did that 25-minute Zhonya really win you the game, or was it wasted gold?

We will implement a module in the Self-Coaching section that will analyze the power impact (Delta) of your purchases. You will be able to see mathematically which shop decisions have a real correlation with your win rate.
